# Brief — cyber-bean

**Problem** · Dialling in espresso on a Sage/Breville Barista machine means remembering the exact settings that made a great shot for each bean — grind size, dose, yield, time, basket — and those get lost between bags. **Audience/ICP** · A single user (the operator himself); a home barista, no other users ever. **Wedge** · A fast, mobile-web dial-in logbook you open mid-brew, organised by bean brand + type, so last-known-good settings are one tap away. **MVP In** · Mobile web app, no auth, no accounts; catalogue of beans (brand + bean type); per-shot dial-in card recording grind size, dose in (g), yield out (g), extraction time, basket type, taste rating (★1–5 + too sour/bitter/balanced), free-text notes; a built-in shot timer (start/stop, feeds the extraction-time field); history per bean so you can see and repeat your best shot; local persistence. **MVP Out** · Auth/multi-user, sharing, cloud sync, social, recommendations/AI, hardware integration, payments.
