# cyber-bean

cyber-bean is a personal, single-user mobile-web espresso dial-in logbook for a Sage/Breville Barista machine — no auth, no accounts, just the owner. For each bean (brand + type) it logs the settings that make a great shot: grind size, dose in (g), yield out (g), extraction time, basket type, a taste rating (★1–5 plus too-sour/bitter/balanced) and free-text notes, with a built-in shot timer feeding the time field and per-bean history to repeat your best pull. It's opened mid-brew, so it must be glanceable and big-tap-friendly. Tone is techno-utilitarian and terse, like a cockpit HUD. The ONE visual north star: the video game *Cyberpunk 2077* — near-black base, high-voltage neon-yellow accents (cyan/magenta secondaries), angular bracketed data panels, subtle scan-lines and glitch on state changes. Absolutely not a light, pastel, rounded SaaS look and never black-and-white minimalism; it should feel like a piece of Night City tech.
