# Brand & design — cyber-bean

**Tone & voice** · Techno-utilitarian, terse, HUD-like — labels read like a cockpit readout ('GRIND','DOSE','YIELD','TIME'), microcopy is clipped and confident. **Colour direction** · Cyberpunk 2077 palette: near-black base (#0a0a0a / deep charcoal), high-voltage neon yellow (#FCEE0A) as the primary accent, electric cyan and hot-magenta as secondaries, with subtle scan-line/glitch texture. **Typography feel** · Monospace or techno display type for numbers and labels (data-panel energy), clean sans for body; large, glanceable numerics on the dial-in card. **Look-and-feel DO** · Dark immersive UI, glowing neon edges, angular/bracketed panels, glitch accents on state changes, big tappable targets for mid-brew use, the timer as a bold glowing readout. **DON'T** · No light/white backgrounds, no soft pastel or rounded-friendly SaaS look, no black-and-white minimalism, no clutter — it must feel like a piece of Night City tech, not a generic notes app.
